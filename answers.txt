Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git's good at tracking and merging changes, even by multiple collaborators.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Any field where managing big data is important, like a job at the US Census Bureau.


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the history from both branches, whereas rebasing applies the changes of the new branch on top of the other, making a linear project history.
